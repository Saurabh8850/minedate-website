Refund & Cookie Policy

Simple, transparent payments — and how we use cookies to make Minedate work well.

Effective date: 30 June 2026

Before you pay

Every purchase clearly shows, before payment:

  • The features included
  • The plan duration (for subscriptions)
  • The total price

Digital goods

Premium subscriptions, boosts, coins, and virtual gifts are digital products delivered immediately. As they are consumed on use, they are generally non-refundable once activated, except as required by law or your app store policy (Apple App Store / Google Play).

When we will help

Contact support if:

  • Your payment succeeded but the feature did not activate
  • You were charged more than once for the same purchase
  • A technical error prevented you from receiving what you paid for

Share your payment ID at support@minedate.app and we will review eligible cases and resolve them promptly.

How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method. Purchases made through the App Store or Google Play are subject to their refund processes. Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider.

Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you securely signed in
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Measure performance and improve the app
  • Understand aggregate, anonymous usage

You can manage device-level permissions (such as notifications and location) from your phone settings at any time.
