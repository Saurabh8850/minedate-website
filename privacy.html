Privacy Policy

Your privacy is central to Minedate. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have over your information.

Effective date: 30 June 2026

On this page Information we collectHow we use itLocationSharingRetention & deletionYour rightsChildren's privacy

Information we collect

To provide and improve the service we collect:

  • Account details: phone number, name, date of birth, gender
  • Profile content: bio, interests, and photos you upload
  • Approximate location, to suggest nearby matches
  • Usage and device data that helps with safety and performance

Your phone number and other private details are never shown publicly.

How we use your information

  • To create your profile and power discovery and matching
  • To enable chat, calls, and other features between matches
  • To process payments for premium features
  • To detect abuse, fraud, and keep the community safe
  • To send notifications you have opted into

Location

Location is used only to show approximate distance to potential matches. We never display your exact location, address, or live movement, and you can control location access from your device settings at any time.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (hosting, payments, messaging)
  • Authorities where required by law or to prevent harm

Data retention & deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which removes your profile, photos, matches, and messages. Some records may be retained where required for legal or security reasons.

Your rights

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Control notification and location permissions
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Contact us with privacy questions at support@minedate.app

Children's privacy

Minedate is strictly for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 and will remove such accounts if discovered.
