Guidelines & Safety

Real people. Real connections. A few simple principles and safety tools keep Minedate welcoming for everyone.

Effective date: 30 June 2026

Be respectful

Treat everyone with kindness. We do not tolerate:

  • Offensive, hateful, or harassing messages
  • Discrimination of any kind
  • Unwanted or threatening behaviour

Be authentic

Your profile should represent the real you:

  • Use recent, genuine photos of yourself
  • Do not impersonate others or use false information

Keep it safe & legal

  • No nudity or sexual content involving minors
  • No spam, scams, or solicitation
  • No promotion of violence or illegal activity

Account & profile privacy

  • Secure phone OTP sign-in — no passwords to leak
  • Encrypted connections between the app and our servers
  • Your phone number is never shown to other users
  • Only approximate distance is displayed — never your exact location

Chat safety

  • Only matched users can message each other
  • Block anyone instantly from the chat menu
  • Report inappropriate behaviour — reports stay private and are reviewed by our safety team
  • Verified badges help you connect with confidence

Block & report

If someone makes you uncomfortable, open their profile or chat and tap Block or Report. Our safety team reviews reports and takes action. Serious violations may be reported to the authorities.

Safe dating tips

  • Keep early conversations inside the app
  • Never share financial information or send money
  • Meet in public places and tell someone you trust
  • Trust your instincts — leave if something feels off
